Laboratory tests

Complete Blood Count

This test is also known as a CBC.It measures the types and numbers of cells in the blood, including red and white blood cells and platelets.

Hemoglobin A1C

This test is used to diagnose and monitor diabetes.

Urinalysis

This is a general screening test used to check for early signs of disease.

Thyroid Stimulating Hormone

This test screens and monitors the function of the thyroid.

Metabolic Panel

This test measures glucose,sodium, potassium, calcium, chloride, blood urea nitrogen which help's to determine blood sugar level

Prothrombin Time

This test is Also known as PT and Pro Time,it measures how long it takes blood to clot.

Cultures

Cultures are used to test for diagnosis and treatment of infections.

Lipid Panel

The lipid panel is a group of tests used to evaluate cardiac risk.
